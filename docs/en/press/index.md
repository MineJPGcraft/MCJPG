# AIJPG Organization Column

## Introduction
Welcome to the homepage of the **AIJPG** Organization Column.  
Here, you can learn about the history and events of the **AIJPG** organization through our column.  
These can help everyone better understand this community and organization.  

## Nature
This article is written by the **AIJPG** publicity department, but anyone can help us enhance and add content to the column. If you wish to contribute, you can visit the **AIJPG** [website repository](https://github.com/MineJPGcraft/AIJPG "Website Repository") to make a PR. We also welcome exchanges and submissions in the community group.  
The publication of the column relies on contributions from **everyone**!
